Cloud Management as Service

Our Managed Cloud Services (MCS) framework assists clients in constructing and leasing back their IT infrastructure. By leveraging our MCS framework, customers can swiftly capitalize on go-to-market opportunities, optimize costs, and focus on business performance. By investing in a shared IT infrastructure with other customers, our MCS contracts enable efficient utilization of on-premise networks and hardware.

Not all IT businesses and workloads may be ideally suited to Public Cloud solutions. This includes scenarios such as handling massive databases with high transaction rates, such as those used in Oracle and SAP HANA, as well as applications requiring low latency, like certain business applications. Another challenge may arise with large network bandwidth usage required between customer locations and the cloud. Additionally, on-premise business applications running on IBM Mainframes and AS400s may not integrate seamlessly with cloud solutions. Finally, the significant cost of running on-premises workloads on the cloud can also be a constraining for some businesses.

Our MCS framework provides the convenience of centrally managing both on-premise and public clouds from a single control portal, offering numerous benefits:

  • The framework enables customers to migrate workloads effortlessly between public and private clouds.
  • During peak workload demands, MCS allows customers to run their burst workloads on other on-premise data centers.
  • New customers can effortlessly onboard their workloads on shared IT infrastructure at their desired locations.
