IT Management as Service

Dynato offers round-the-clock support services for the complete IT infrastructure and cloud operations, utilizing our extensive teams both offshore and onsite. We work in partnership with our clients' IT infrastructure and application teams to manage ongoing level-2 and level-3 operations and maintenance activities. In addition, our clients can benefit from the on-demand implementation team's service, which can be used to design and scale their IT infrastructure both in the cloud and on-premises.

Our offshore and onsite teams possess comprehensive expertise spanning from legacy to cutting-edge IT technologies and applications. Clients have the option to select IMaS team members based on their skillsets to operate their IT infrastructures. Our services enable clients to allocate their resources away from routine IT maintenance activities and concentrate on modernizing, scaling, and transforming their IT applications to meet future business requirements.

Our IT management as a Service provides several additional benefits, including:

  • Ensuring security and regulatory compliance through timely system upgrades.
  • Ability to handle modernized and scaled applications, as well as systems of any size.
  • Flexible pricing options, including fixed and variable rates.
  • Support for a diverse range of ERP and BI applications that operate in both on-premises and cloud environments.
